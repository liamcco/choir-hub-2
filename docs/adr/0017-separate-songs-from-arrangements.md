# Separate Songs from Arrangements

CSK Choir Hub will model a Song as the identity of a musical work and an Arrangement as a specific scored version of that Song. A single Song may therefore have both SATB and TTBB Arrangements, and the Arrangement—not the Song—owns whether its scoring uses an undivided Voice Type such as B or refinements such as B1 and B2.

This prevents repertoire identity, arrangement-specific resources, and singable parts from being duplicated or conflated when the same work is prepared for different choir configurations.
