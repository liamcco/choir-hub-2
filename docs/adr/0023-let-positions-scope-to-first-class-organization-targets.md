# Let Positions scope to first-class organization targets

CSK Choir Hub will allow a Position Scope to reference CSK, a Choir, a Section, or a Group directly. A Position may have multiple Scopes where one office genuinely spans several targets, but Choirs and Sections will not be recreated as Groups merely to support Position scoping.

This supports organization-wide officers, choir-specific offices, Voice Parents, and committee or Board roles with the same Position model. A Voice Parent Assignment is valid only while its holder has a Section Placement in at least one of the Position's scoped Sections. Choir-scoped Master of Concerts and Master of Gigs Assignments likewise require a matching Choir Membership throughout, while Conductor Assignments are exempt because a Conductor need not sing in the Choir. Group-scoped Position Assignments also supply the dated effective Group Membership established in ADR-0024.
